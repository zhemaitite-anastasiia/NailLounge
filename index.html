Zoya's Nails was founded in 2017 and is based in Chicago, Illinois. We know how difficult it can be to find the "right" Nail Salon, and here are a couple of reasons as to why you should choose us. We are dedicated to ensuring that our customer's nails are healthy by sourcing the best nail polish brands, hard gels, and acrygel. Our experienced nail techs are committed providing you with an exceptional nail service at a reasonable price. Our build-your-own style menu will allow you to customize your nail art with the numerous of options and to your liking.  

By choosing Zoya's Nails you can be certain that your service will be full of care and love, because we pay attention to every detail and we love what we do!
